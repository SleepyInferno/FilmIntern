**Prompt Updates** -- Append a new section to each prompt's system string (BEFORE the closing backtick). Add AFTER the existing `## Rules:` section in each prompt:

**narrative.ts** -- Append:
```
### Overall Assessment
- Provide an overall quality score from 1-10 (use decimals like 7.5 for precision)
- Write a 2-3 sentence summary that captures the script's key strengths and most critical areas for improvement
- Be calibrated: a 5 is average, 7+ is strong, 9+ is exceptional. Most scripts should land between 5-8.
```

**documentary.ts** -- Append:
```
### Subject Profiles
- For each distinct interviewee/subject, create a brief profile: their name or identifier, their role in the story, their key contribution to the documentary, and their quotability rating
- Rate quotability: highly-quotable (natural on camera, memorable phrasing), adequate (usable but not standout), needs-coaching (awkward delivery or off-topic)

### Story Arc Assessment
- Assess the overall narrative arc potential from this interview material
- Suggest a documentary structure (chronological, thematic, character-driven, parallel narratives, etc.)
- Identify arc strengths and gaps where additional material would be needed

### Overall Assessment
- Provide an overall quality score from 1-10 (use decimals like 7.5 for precision)
- Write a 2-3 sentence summary that captures the material's key strengths and most critical gaps
- Be calibrated: a 5 is average, 7+ is strong, 9+ is exceptional.
```

**corporate.ts** -- Append:
```
### Spokesperson Assessment
- Provide an overall assessment of spokesperson readiness: camera-ready, needs-coaching, or significant-prep-needed
- Summarize the overall performance across all speakers
- Identify the top performer if there are multiple speakers

### Audience Alignment
- Identify the target audience for this corporate content
- Rate how well the messaging aligns with that audience: strong, adequate, or weak
- Suggest specific ways to better align messaging with audience expectations

### Message Consistency
- Rate overall message consistency across speakers: unified, mostly-consistent, mixed-signals, or contradictory
- Assess how well key messages are reinforced across the material
- Note any specific messaging conflicts or contradictions

### Overall Assessment
- Provide an overall quality score from 1-10 (use decimals like 7.5 for precision)
- Write a 2-3 sentence summary of the material quality and readiness
- Be calibrated: a 5 is average, 7+ is strong, 9+ is exceptional.
```

**tv-episodic.ts** -- Append:
```
### Tone & Voice
- Describe the dominant tone of the show
- Assess voice consistency: distinctive (unique and ownable), consistent (reliable tone), uneven (shifts without purpose), undefined (no clear voice)
- Provide tonal comparisons to 1-2 existing shows if helpful

### Pilot Effectiveness
- Assess world-building: immersive, adequate, or insufficient
- Assess character establishment: compelling, adequate, or flat
- Assess hook strength: strong, adequate, or weak
- Provide overall pilot effectiveness evaluation

### Franchise Potential
- Rate franchise/IP expansion potential: high, moderate, or limited
- Assess opportunities for spinoffs, IP extension, or expanded universe
- Be honest about whether the concept has legs beyond the initial series

### Overall Assessment
- Provide an overall quality score from 1-10 (use decimals like 7.5 for precision)
- Write a 2-3 sentence summary capturing the show's strengths and critical areas
- Be calibrated: a 5 is average, 7+ is strong, 9+ is exceptional.
```

**short-form.ts** -- Append:
```
### Audience Fit
- Identify the primary target audience for this content
- Rate audience fit: strong, adequate, or weak
- Assess whether the content's tone, messaging, and format match audience expectations
- Suggest specific improvements for better audience alignment

### Overall Assessment
- Provide an overall quality score from 1-10 (use decimals like 7.5 for precision)
- Write a 2-3 sentence summary of the content's effectiveness
- Be calibrated: a 5 is average, 7+ is strong, 9+ is exceptional.
```
